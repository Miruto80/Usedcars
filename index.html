How to Apply for Financing with Us

Basic Requirements

  • Valid Identification: Driver’s license or state-issued ID.
  • Proof of Income: Recent pay stubs, bank statements, or an employer verification letter.
  • Proof of Residence: Utility bills, lease agreement, or bank statement.
  • Down Payment: An initial amount based on the vehicle selected.

Application Process

  1. Select Your Vehicle: Browse our inventory and choose the car that best suits your needs.
  2. Submit Your Application: Complete a quick application online or in person. Our team will review your documents and provide you with financing options.
  3. Fast and Flexible Approval:
    • Buy Here, Pay Here (BHPH): Direct in-house financing no perfect credit required.
    • Bank Financing: We work with trusted financial institutions to offer competitive rates.
  4. Sign and Drive: Once approved, review and sign your contract. You’ll drive away in your new vehicle the same day.
